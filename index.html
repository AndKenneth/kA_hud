Welcome to kA_hud

kA_hud (kah-hud) is a Team Fortress 2 hud based on the old discontinued version of yA_hud. It is designed to have a simple look while still maintaining a visual language that informs the player of what they need when they need it.

kA_hud is currently in an early release stage and will be updated often.
